DEHA ONE lets you connect your AI agents to the messaging platforms your customers already use. Set up once, and the same agent handles conversations across every connected channel -- no extra configuration needed.

## One Agent, Every Channel

When you build an AI agent in DEHA ONE, it works the same way regardless of how your customer reaches out. Whether someone sends a WhatsApp message, a Telegram chat, an email, or uses your website's chat widget, the same agent responds with the same quality and knowledge.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Customer sends a message">
    A customer reaches out through any connected channel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your AI agent responds">
    DEHA ONE routes the message to your configured agent. The agent processes the message, looks up relevant information from your knowledge base, and generates a response.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reply is delivered">
    The response is sent back to the customer on the same channel they used to reach out.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Extensible Channel Architecture

DEHA ONE's channel system is designed to be **open and extensible**. The five built-in channels are just the starting point -- any messaging platform, communication tool, or custom interface can be connected as a new channel.

<Info>
  Every channel normalizes inbound traffic into a single internal **CloudEvent** format. Your agents, automations, and routing rules work with any channel without modification. Adding a new channel is purely a translation problem.
</Info>

## Setting Up Channels

You can enable and configure channels from the DEHA ONE dashboard. Each channel has its own setup guide:

1. **Go to Settings** in your dashboard
2. **Select Channels** from the menu
3. **Enable** the channels you want to use
4. **Follow the setup steps** for each channel (linking your WhatsApp Business account, Telegram bot, email provider, etc.)

<Info>
  All credentials (API tokens, passwords, secrets) are stored securely and encrypted. You never need to worry about them being exposed.
</Info>

## How It All Connects

Once a channel is set up, everything works automatically:

- **Conversations are tracked** across channels, so you can see all customer interactions in one place
- **Your agent uses the same knowledge** regardless of the channel
- **Human review** works the same way -- if an agent flags a response for review, your team sees it in the dashboard no matter which channel the customer used
- **Usage and billing** are tracked per channel, so you can see which channels are most active
- **New channels integrate seamlessly** -- when you add a new channel, all your existing agents and automations work with it immediately
