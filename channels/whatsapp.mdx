Connect your DEHA ONE agents to WhatsApp so your customers can get instant, intelligent responses through the messaging app they already use every day.

## What You Need

Before you start, make sure you have:

1. A [Meta Business account](https://business.facebook.com/)
2. A WhatsApp Business app created in the [Meta Developer Dashboard](https://developers.facebook.com/)
3. A verified phone number for WhatsApp Business
4. Your App Secret, Phone Number ID, and a System User access token from Meta

<Info>
  If you do not have a Meta Business account yet, follow [Meta's guide](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/1710077379203657) to create one. The process takes about 10 minutes.
</Info>

## Setup Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the DEHA ONE Dashboard">
    Go to **Settings > Channels > WhatsApp** and click **Connect WhatsApp**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your Meta credentials">
    You will need to provide:
    - **Phone Number ID** -- Found in your Meta Developer Dashboard under WhatsApp > Getting Started
    - **API Token** -- A System User access token with WhatsApp messaging permissions
    - **App Secret** -- Found in your Meta App Dashboard under Settings > Basic
    - **Verify Token** -- A custom token you create (any string you choose)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the webhook in Meta">
    In your Meta Developer Dashboard, go to **WhatsApp > Configuration** and set the webhook URL to:

    ```
    https://api.deha.one/webhook/{your_user_id}/whatsapp
    ```

    Enter the same **Verify Token** you provided in DEHA ONE. Subscribe to the **messages** webhook field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your agent">
    Select which AI agent should handle WhatsApp conversations. You can also set an optional welcome message that greets new customers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send a test message">
    Send a message to your WhatsApp Business number from your personal phone. You should receive an AI-generated response within seconds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Your Customers Can Send

Your AI agent can receive and understand the following types of messages from WhatsApp users:

| Message Type | Description |
|---|---|
| Text | Regular text messages |
| Images | Photos with optional captions |
| Documents | PDFs, spreadsheets, and other files |
| Audio / Voice notes | Audio recordings |
| Video | Video files |
| Location | Shared GPS coordinates |
| Button replies | When a customer taps a button from a template |
| List selections | When a customer picks an item from a list |

Your agent responds with text messages. Support for rich message templates (images, buttons, lists) in outbound messages is coming soon.

## Tips and Best Practices

<Tip>
  WhatsApp users expect quick, conversational replies. Configure your agent's personality to be concise and friendly rather than formal or long-winded.
</Tip>

### Respond within 24 hours

WhatsApp allows free-form messaging only within 24 hours of the customer's last message. After that window closes, you can only send pre-approved message templates. Since DEHA ONE responds instantly, this is usually not an issue.

### Keep your quality rating high

Meta monitors how customers interact with your business. If too many users block or report your number, Meta may reduce your messaging limits. To maintain a good rating:

- Make sure your agent provides helpful, relevant responses
- Do not send unsolicited messages
- Let customers know they are chatting with an AI assistant

### Phone number formatting

DEHA ONE automatically handles phone number formatting. Whether a customer's number comes in as `+90 532 123 4567` or `90-532-123-4567`, the platform normalizes it correctly.

### Messaging limits

New WhatsApp Business accounts start with a limit of 1,000 unique users per 24 hours. As your quality rating improves, Meta automatically increases this limit. Most active businesses reach unlimited messaging within a few weeks.

<Warning>
  Keep your API credentials secure. Never share your App Secret or API Token publicly. DEHA ONE stores all credentials in an encrypted vault.
</Warning>
