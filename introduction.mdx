## Why DEHA ONE

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Build with natural language">
    DEHA, your built-in AI assistant, lets you describe what you want in plain English. It generates the configuration, validates it, deploys it, runs tests, and auto-heals if something fails. No code required for 95% of use cases.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Works with any AI model">
    Use OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, DeepSeek, or your own self-hosted models via LiteLLM. Pick a model per agent, swap models without rebuilding, or use semantic aliases like `fast`, `smart`, or `cheap` and let the platform route.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Bring Your Own Data (BYOD)">
    Your business data stays in your warehouse. DEHA ONE processes data but does not persist it -- results sink back to your PostgreSQL, MySQL, MongoDB, S3, or external Qdrant. Optional internal storage for operational data only.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Bring Your Own Keys (BYOK)">
    Use your own LLM provider credentials (OpenAI, Vertex AI with your GCP service account, Anthropic, etc.). Embeddings, completions, and tools run on your accounts with your rate limits and your billing relationship.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Enterprise-grade security">
    Zero-Trust gateway, Auth0 SSO, role-based access control, per-user data isolation, encrypted vault for credentials, IP whitelisting, signed webhooks, and a full audit trail.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Transparent, credit-based pricing">
    Pay only for what you use. Monitor your credit consumption in real time, set quota alerts, and control spending with configurable overage actions (alert, throttle, or block).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Multi-user, multi-region, hot-reloadable">
    Every change deploys atomically across the cluster -- no restart, no downtime. Run multiple versions of an agent with weighted traffic splits. A/B test in production safely.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
