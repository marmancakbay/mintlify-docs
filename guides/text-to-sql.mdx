## What You Will Learn

Use natural language to ask questions about your business data and get instant, accurate answers -- no SQL knowledge required.

<Info>
Before you begin, make sure you have:
- Data loaded into your workspace (see [Connect a Data Source](/guides/connect-data-source))
- At least one table available for querying
</Info>

---

<Steps>

### Ask a Question

You can ask questions about your data in two ways:

**Through your agent (any channel):**

Just ask your agent a data question in natural language through chat, WhatsApp, Telegram, or any connected channel:

> "What are our top 10 customers by total order value?"

> "How many support tickets were opened last month?"

> "Show me the revenue trend for the past 6 months."

The agent automatically converts your question to a database query, runs it, and explains the results conversationally.

**Through the dashboard:**

1. Go to **Data > Query** in the dashboard.
2. Type your question in the search bar.
3. View the results as a table, chart, or summary.

### Improve Accuracy with Training

Help the system understand your business terminology by providing examples and definitions. Go to **Data > Query > Training** in the dashboard.

**Add business definitions:**

Teach the system what your terms mean:

> "An active customer is one who has placed at least one order in the last 90 days."

> "Churn rate is the percentage of customers who were active 90 days ago but are no longer active."

**Add example questions:**

Provide sample questions with the correct answers to improve accuracy for common queries your team asks.

<Tip>
The more training examples you provide, the more accurate the results become. Start with 5-10 common questions your team asks regularly.
</Tip>

### Choose a Result Format

When querying through the dashboard or API, you can choose how results are displayed:

| Format | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Table** | Structured rows and columns |
| **Summary** | A natural language explanation of the results |
| **JSON** | Raw data for programmatic use |

### Run Direct SQL (Advanced)

If you know SQL, you can run queries directly in the dashboard under **Data > Query > SQL Editor**. Only `SELECT` statements are allowed for security.

<Check>
Ask your agent a question about your data and verify you get an accurate answer. Try the same question in the dashboard query tool to compare results.
</Check>

</Steps>

---

## Tips for Better Results

- **Be specific.** "What were total sales last quarter?" works better than "Tell me about sales."
- **Use your business terms.** If you have added training definitions, the system understands terms like "active customer" or "churn rate."
- **Start simple.** Begin with straightforward questions and build up to complex ones.
- **Check the generated query.** In the dashboard, you can see the SQL that was generated from your question. This helps you verify accuracy.

## Safety and Security

- All queries are **read-only** -- your source data cannot be modified through DEHA ONE.
- Each workspace can only query its own data -- there is no cross-workspace access.
- Query complexity is monitored to prevent expensive operations.
- Row limits are enforced (default: 10,000 rows) to keep results manageable.
